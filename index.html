Welcome Traveler!

Right about now you are probably just asking yourself what you are looking at. Well, Im glad you asked. This is a state of the art, one of a kind Choose-Your-Own-Adventure-Quiz™. It is a project designed and written by a single author with the express purpose of providing a narrative driven quiz covering several diverse survival scenarios. For more information about the author and his university, please navigate to the "About Us" section. There is a persistent scoring system to grade the choices you make as you navigate the different choices and scenarios you may find yourself in. In the event that you find yourself in a sticky situation you will have the option to restart the scenario and begin anew. So, without further ado get comfortable, put your thinking cap on and be ready to choose to survive.
