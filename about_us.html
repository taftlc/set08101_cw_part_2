Who is this mysterious web developer?

Luke Taft is a 2nd year student at Edinburgh Napier University and is earning his Bachelor's Degree in Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics. He is 30 years old and a native of the greatest country on earth: the United States of America. A veteran of the United States Marine Corps, Mr. Taft has worked in the information technology industry for the past 6 years. His current educational pursuits are in service of a career shift from network administration to cybersecurity. Mr. Taft has lived in Edinburgh for the past 2 years following his acceptance to Edinburgh Napier University. Before this venture, Mr. Taft was employed as an international field technician in the South Korean satellite office of Advantor Systems. His free time is occupied by his interests, which include precision sports, weight lifting, camping, hiking, scotch, and woodworking.
