Who is this mysterious web developer?

Luke Taft is a 2nd year student at Edinburgh Napier University and is earning his Bachelor's Degree in Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics. He is 30 years old and a native of the greatest country on earth: the United States of America. A veteran of the United States Marine Corps, Mr. Taft has worked in the information technology industry for the past 6 years. His current educational pursuits are in service of a career shift from network administration to cybersecurity. Mr. Taft has lived in Edinburgh for the past 2 years following his acceptance to Edinburgh Napier University. Before this venture, Mr. Taft was employed as an international field technician in the South Korean satellite office of Advantor Systems. While employed at Advantor Systems Mr Taft acted as an installer/maintainer for physical security systems aboard US Military installations in South Korea and Japan. His free time is occupied by his interests, which include precision sports, weight lifting, camping, hiking, scotch, and woodworking.


Edinburgh Napier University is a university located in Edinburgh Scotland. Their School Of Computing campus is located in Merchiston (pronounced "merk-is-ton") and is the campus where instruction in the Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics field is held. The Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics program is a 4 year degree plan which results in a Bachelor of Engineering degree with the possibility of an Honors degree. The program has its main focus in security and forensics, software development and computer networks.
